Security

Learn the difference between owning bitcoin and trusting someone else to hold it.

Self-custody is one of the biggest conceptual jumps for newcomers. This page focuses on wallets, keys, backups, and scam prevention so readers understand the responsibility before they rush in.

Core concepts

  • Your wallet manages keys, not coins sitting in an account.
  • Backups matter because Bitcoin transactions are irreversible.
  • Scam awareness is just as important as price awareness.
