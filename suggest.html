Suggestions

Suggest missing information, corrections, or new public sources.

Use this page to flag missing lawmakers, public statements, bills, votes, broken links, or outdated summaries. Submissions open a prefilled GitHub issue so the record can be reviewed and updated.

What helps most

  • Name the lawmaker or bill as clearly as possible.
  • Include the direct source URL when you have one.
  • Explain why the current site looks incomplete or wrong.

Submit an update

Structured suggestions are easier to review quickly.

Good submissions include

  • A direct official source whenever possible.
  • Whether the issue is about a senator, representative, bill, vote, or resource link.
  • A short explanation of what should be added or corrected.
  • Why the source is Bitcoin-specific, if that distinction matters.

Clicking submit opens a prefilled GitHub issue in the public project repo. That keeps the correction trail organized without requiring a separate backend form service.
